ELT Support

For additional support, email ELTsupport@oup.com. Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your type of computer (for example, PC or Mac)
  • Your operating system (for example, Windows 8)
  • Your browser (for example, Firefox 17.0.5)
  • A description of the problem, including any specific actions that were taken before the problem occurred.

Recommended Browsers and Supported Devices

Windows®:

Internet Explorer 9+, Firefox, Chrome

Flash Player 11+ plug-in is required for recording activities

Mac® OS X:

Firefox, Safari 7.0.2+, Chrome

Flash Player 11+ plug-in is required for recording activities

Android®:

OS must be 4.0 or higher and supported browser is Google® Chrome

Tested and supported on the following devices: Samsung® Galaxy Tab v2 & v3, Samsung® Galaxy Note 10.1 7” & 10”, Google® Nexus 7, Google® Nexus 10

iOS®:

OS must be iOS®6 or higher and the supported browser is Safari

Tested and supported on the following devices: iPad® 2 or later, iPad® Mini, iPad® Air

* Recording activities on Windows® and Mac® OSX require Flash Player 11+ plug-in. Go to the following site to download the latest version of Flash Player: http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/
